SaleBEE Privacy Policy

V1.0

Release Date: 12th March 2017

Introduction

SaleBEE values the privacy of its users and subscribers. Using SaleBEE may require some information to be collected from you and used accordingly to provide you the service. Without these required information, you will not be able to register or use SaleBEE app. In addition, this information helps us to provide you a better experience and avail you the full functionality of our services. We have developed this privacy policy to reflect the aspects of how we collect and use your information so that you understand the privacy related practices. This privacy policy applies to users of SaleBEE mobile application in Bangladesh.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

We define Personal Information as the set of information that identifies you as a person. We collect your Name, Email, Phone Number and password during the sign up process. Your phone number, and email address, along with the password works as the access credential. You can use the credential to have a synchronized experience across multiple devices. Once you are signed in, the app will be allowed to access your location, phone, photo/media/files, calendar, camera, wi-fi connection, mobile data connection & sms. Any personal information you provide is not disclosed or transferred in any form to any entity other than SaleBEE team and Nexzen Solution Ltd. We do not use any information for purposes other than aforementioned practices.

Collection and Use of Other Information

Other information provided by you will be collected through the app if you use following sections:

  • Profile: All the profile details and images you provide.
  • Camera: Pictures for profile or shops/outlets/products. These data are collected to sync your information across multiple devices.

In-app Permissions

  • Direct Phone Call Access is required to make hotline calls.
  • Full Network Access is used to send and receive data for sign-up, sign-in, downloading/streaming content, sync across devices etc.
  • Phone Storage Read/Write is used to update images and insert image in the personal profile and/or to various modules of the application.
  • GPS for location and tracking.
  • Calendar for meeting schedule.
  • Wi-Fi Connection for connectivity
  • Mobile Data for connectivity
